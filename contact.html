Frequently Asked Questions

You can book a trek by visiting the trek detail page and clicking the "Book Now" button. You'll be guided through the booking process including selecting your batch date, entering participant details, and making payment.

We offer flexible cancellation with full refund if cancelled 30+ days before the trek, 50% refund for 15-30 days, and 25% refund for 7-14 days. No refund is available for cancellations less than 7 days before the trek.

Fitness requirements vary by trek difficulty. Easy treks require basic fitness, moderate treks need regular exercise routine, and difficult treks require good cardiovascular fitness. We recommend starting training 4-6 weeks before your trek.
