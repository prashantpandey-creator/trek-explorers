About Trek Explorers

Connecting adventurers with the majestic Himalayas since 2015

Our Story

Trek Explorers India was born from a simple belief: that the mountains have the power to transform lives. Founded by a group of passionate trekkers in 2015, we started with a dream to make Himalayan adventures accessible to everyone.

What began as weekend treks with friends has grown into a community of over 15,000 trekkers who share our love for the outdoors. We've led expeditions across Uttarakhand, Himachal Pradesh, Ladakh, Kashmir, and beyond—always with safety, sustainability, and unforgettable experiences at our core.
